Welcome to Lifebyte

Welcome to Lifebyte a minstry which is part of 'Colorado Community Church'

Lifebyte is composed of a group of spirit-filled people who use the gifts and direction of God to acquire, restore, and deliver free computers while sharing the love of Jesus Christ. Lifebyte share the message and love of Jesus Christ by rebuilding, deploying, and maintaining donated computers across the Denver Metro Area and beyond.

Lifebyte ministry is reaching commmunity through Computers for the past 10 years. Maintaining, fixing, donating Computers to the community is the main function of the Lifebyte. Donations in the form of Computers and/or financial will be accepted.

Administrative Team

Contact Point Person

Administrative Team is the face of the Lifebyte Ministry. This Team co-ordinates all activities of this ministry. The primary responsibility of thie Team is to attend/check voice mails, updating waiting list, communicating with CCC Staff.

Equipment Team

Contact Point Person

Equipment Team is responsible for Reusing/Refurbishing the items that are kept at the ministry storage unit.

 

Build Team

Contact Point Person

Build Team is responsible for Maintaining/updating “PC Processing Guide” document, Remain abreast of best free software used for build standards, Creation of build disks used for PC refurbishment, Creation of bootable & tools disks used at PC WashRefurbish / build computers (group leader)

 

Repair Team

Contact Point Person

Repair Team is responsible for Contact recipients who require repair and facilitate the completion of the repair, Phone/In Person (Pickup), Coordinate with the Build Team when unrepairable computers require replacement, Update pre existing Lifebyte database information / LB numbers as needed
